About me

Hello, I’m Kat Wlodarczyk. Ditital Designer and Web Developer.
Originally from Poland but living permanently in UK since 2013.
(Bsc) Digital Design and Web Development student on Solent Univeristy.
Multi-tasker and work-a-holic. Perfectionist. Photography lover.

Always in pursuit to learn something new and to create products that are both beautiful and useful.

How it all started?

I wrote my first lines of code when I was about 11 and needed a theme for a blog. It was a silly Paint-made theme with a code written in NotePad, but I will never forget that feeling of pride and joy when I finished and it worked!
I still get this feeling everytime I design and develop.



I'm a content creator.

Since September 2019 I write about my dev journey on a blog: katcodes.home.blog, and some of my articles have already been published via third parties. Recently I also started creating educational videos, like tutorials or talks, and publish them on Youtube.
